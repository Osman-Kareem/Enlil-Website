Who We Are

The Enlil Center for Environment and Sustainable Development is a non-profit, independent think tank based in Iraq. We conduct applied research, provide policy analysis, and promote evidence-based approaches to environmental governance, climate resilience, and sustainable development.

Our work focuses on climate change, water and energy security, environmental health, and the social and economic impacts of environmental degradation.

We partner with Iraqi institutions, civil society, and international organizations to translate research into practical, locally anchored solutions.

"Our mission is to bridge rigorous scientific research with practical, locally relevant solutions for a more resilient, prosperous Iraq."

  • Independent, non-partisan research and analysis
  • Focus on climate, water, energy and environmental governance
  • Capacity building and policy engagement with Iraqi stakeholders
